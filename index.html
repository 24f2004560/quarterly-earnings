Analyst Query: Basic SQL Example

-- Revenue by product for Q4
SELECT product_id,
       SUM(amount) AS revenue
FROM transactions
WHERE transaction_date BETWEEN '2025-10-01' AND '2025-12-31'
GROUP BY product_id
ORDER BY revenue DESC;
Explain the query at a high-level: filter by date, aggregate revenue, sort. Mention how this integrates with the analytics pipeline.

Financial Metrics

Present Value of expected cash flows (Discounted Cash Flow):

\( \displaystyle PV = \sum_{t=1}^{N} \frac{CF_t}{(1 + r)^t} \)

Where:

  • \(CF_t\) = cash flow at time \(t\)
  • \(r\) = discount rate
Walk through an example: show how a change in discount rate impacts valuation. Use the fragment reveals to focus audience attention.
